## â“ FAQ

**Q: Will auto-save slow down my typing?**
A: No! Auto-save works in the background silently.

**Q: Can I disable auto-save?**
A: Not currently, but it's designed to be non-intrusive.

**Q: How do I know if auto-save worked?**
A: Look for the green "âœ“ Draft saved at HH:MM:SS" notification.

**Q: Can I edit someone else's treatment plan?**
A: No, you can only edit treatment plans for consultations assigned to you.

**Q: Will editing a plan send a new notification to the patient?**
A: No, notifications are only sent when first creating the plan.

**Q: What if patient has no previous history?**
A: The load history button will show "No previous medical history found" - just fill in manually.

**Q: Can I see all of a patient's past consultations?**
A: Currently no, but this feature could be added in the future.
